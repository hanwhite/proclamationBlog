What is The Family: A Proclamation to the World?

“The Family: A Proclamation to the World” was written in 1995 by the First Presidency and the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. It is a declaration of the principles, concepts, and boundaries that are essential to happiness and joy in each family. It can be read in full here.
